# 🌟 PixelPillar: 

This 360° Smart LED display configured using RGB P10 Panels handled via Vega Aries v3. It is completely wireless and controlled entirely through the cloud, meaning you can update your display from your phone remotely.

## 🛠️ Step 1: First-Time Setup (Connecting to Wi-Fi)

1. **Plug it in:** Connect your PixelPillar to a power outlet.
2. **Open your Phone:** Go to the Wi-Fi settings on your smartphone.
3. **Connect to the Pillar:** Look for a new Wi-Fi network named **`PixelPillar_Setup`** and connect to it.
4. **The Pop-Up:** A setup screen will automatically pop up on your phone. Tap **"Configure WiFi"**.
5. **Enter your Password:** Select your Wi-Fi network from the list, type in your Wi-Fi password, and hit **Save**.

*That’s it! The PixelPillar will now connect to the internet. You can switch your phone's Wi-Fi back to normal.*

## 📱 Step 2: How to Change the Screen

1. Open Safari, Chrome, or any web browser on your phone or computer.
2. Go to control panel: **[https://nishil-patel-pixelpillar.hf.space](https://nishil-patel-pixelpillar.hf.space)** 
3. Log in using the default Admin Password: **`led123`**
4. Check the top of the screen. If you see the **🟢 PixelPillar Online** badge, your screen is ready!

## 🎨 Step 3: Uploading Content
Click on a category to get started:

* **🖼️ Images:** Upload any JPG or PNG.
* **🎥 Videos & GIFs:** Upload motion graphics or MP4 videos.
* **✏️ Text:** You can customize the font style, size, and color.

**Additional Features:**
* **Panoramic Scroll:** If your image or text is too wide for the screen, turn on "Enable Panoramic Scroll". The PixelPillar will smoothly wrap your image around the cylinder!
* **Scale (%):** Use the scale slider to zoom in or zoom out on your images so they fit perfectly.

Once you have your content looking good, simply click **Publish** and watch your physical screen update instantly!

## ❓ Troubleshooting

**Q: The dashboard says "🔴 PixelPillar Offline". What do I do?** <br>
A: This means your physical screen cannot reach the internet. Ensure it is plugged into power and that Wi-Fi router/ Mobile Data is turned on. 

**Q: I changed my device's Wi-Fi password, or I moved the Pillar to a new location. How do I reconnect it?** <br>
A: If the PixelPillar cannot find its saved Wi-Fi network, it will automatically broadcast the **`PixelPillar_Setup`** network again. Just repeat Step 1 to give it your new Wi-Fi details!
